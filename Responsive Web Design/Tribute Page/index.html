Avicii

The music legend who inspired millions

Here's a timeline of Avicii's life:

  • 1989 - Born in Stockholm, Sweden
  • 2008 - Begins posting his remixes on electronic music forums
  • 2010 - Releases "Seek Bromance," his first major single
  • 2011 - Achieves international fame with the release of "Levels"
  • 2013 - Releases debut studio album "True," featuring hits like "Wake Me Up"
  • 2015 - Releases second studio album "Stories"
  • 2016 - Announces retirement from touring, citing health reasons
  • 2018 - Tragically passes away at the age of 28
  • 2019 - Posthumous album "Tim" is released

"Tim was a musical genius who touched the lives of millions with his groundbreaking music. His legacy will live on forever."
